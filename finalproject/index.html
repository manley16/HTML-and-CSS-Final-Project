Great Guitarists

  • Jimi Hendrix
  • Eric Clapton
  • Jimmy Page
  • Eddie Van Halen
Throughout history there have been some fantastic guitar players. However you categorize greatness as a professional musician, I believe these are some of the most incredible guitar players of modern music. Personally, I believe Jimi Hendrix is the best guitar player of all time. At number two I place my personal favorite guitarist, Eric Clapton. Third is Jimmy Page, the guitarist from Led Zeppelin. Fourth is Eddie Van Halen, from the band Van Halen.
